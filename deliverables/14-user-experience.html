Guide/Reference/User Experience
CHAPTER 14

WHAT CHANGES FOR YOUR TEAM

8 MIN READ

Consolidation changes the daily experience for every team. Here's what each role will see differently — and how to prepare them.

BEFORE & AFTER BY ROLE

SARAH
SALES REP — PRODUCT A

BEFORE

Only sees Product A contacts. No idea if they also bought Product B. Can't suggest bundles or cross-sell. Each customer looks like a single-product buyer.

AFTER

Full customer timeline showing all products. Cross-sell prompts when a contact owns one product but not another. Can see total customer value across all product lines.

MARCUS
SERVICE MANAGER — PRODUCT B

BEFORE

Creates a new ticket but can't see the customer's Product A history. They have to explain everything from scratch. Resolution takes twice as long because context is missing.

AFTER

Opens a ticket and immediately sees the full history across all products. Previous tickets, past purchases, and all communication — on one timeline. Faster resolution, happier customers.

LISA
MARKETING DIRECTOR

BEFORE

Manages 3 separate email lists. Has to log into each portal to send campaigns. Can't run cross-brand campaigns. Reporting is fragmented — has to manually combine numbers from 3 dashboards.

AFTER

One subscription centre with per-brand preferences. Brand-specific campaigns from one system. Cross-sell campaigns targeting "bought A but not B." Unified analytics dashboard showing all brands side by side.

DAVID
CEO

BEFORE

Asks for total customer count and gets 3 different numbers from 3 portals. Revenue reporting requires manual spreadsheet work. Can't see total lifetime value or cross-sell conversion rates.

AFTER

One dashboard. True unique customer count. Revenue by product line. Total lifetime value per customer. Cross-sell conversion rates. Real-time data, no manual work.

TRAINING PLAN

Schedule training before go-live, not after. People perform better when they know what to expect.

ROLEWHAT THEY NEED TO LEARNTIME
Sales repsNew portal navigation, finding contacts across brands, cross-sell pipeline view2 hours
Service repsUnified ticket view, finding cross-product history, new ticket routing2 hours
Marketing teamBusiness Units, brand-specific campaigns, unified subscription centre, cross-brand reporting4 hours

PRO TIP

Assign a "migration champion" on each team — one person who gets trained first and becomes the go-to for questions in the first two weeks. This prevents the IT team from being overwhelmed with support requests.
