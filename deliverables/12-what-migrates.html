CHAPTER 12

WHAT MIGRATES VS WHAT DOESN'T

6 MIN READ

Not everything in HubSpot can be automatically moved between portals. Understanding what migrates, what needs rebuilding, and what's lost permanently helps you plan the right amount of time and resources.

AUTO-MIGRATES

HANDLED BY THE MIGRATION TOOL

Contacts, Companies, Deals, Tickets, Custom Objects, Properties (all custom fields), Associations (all links between records), Lists, Forms (structure only), Workflows, Marketing Emails, Pipelines, Users/Owners, Engagements (meetings, tasks, notes, calls, emails, SMS), Files & Attachments, Line Items & Products, Currencies, Deal Splits.

MUST BE MANUALLY RECREATED

REBUILD IN THE TARGET PORTAL

Reports & Dashboards, CTAs, Landing Page Designs, Website Pages, Blog Posts, Email Template Designs, Knowledge Base Articles, Chatflows, Meeting Links, Custom Surveys, Brand Kits, Playbooks, Snippets, Saved Views, Team Structure, Permissions Configuration.

PLANNING TIP

This is the most time-consuming part of migration. Budget 1-2 weeks for rebuilding reports, dashboards, and templates — especially if you have complex custom reports.

LOST PERMANENTLY

CANNOT BE MIGRATED OR RECREATED

Marketing email campaign performance data (historical open rates, click rates, engagement metrics), Historical web traffic analytics, Properties with the same internal name but different field types across portals.

BEFORE MIGRATION

Screenshot or export your best-performing email campaign data. The campaigns themselves migrate, but their performance metrics don't. Keep records of your top subject lines, open rates, and click rates for reference.
