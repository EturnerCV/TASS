THE FULL PROCESS

Every step from first conversation to ongoing support. Scroll down to see the complete journey.

01
ENGAGE HUBSPOT PARTNER
FIRST POINT OF CALL

Before anything else, engage a certified HubSpot Solutions Partner. They have direct access to HubSpot's migration support, can negotiate Enterprise pricing, and have done this before. A partner de-risks the entire project.

HUBSPOT PARTNER
02
AI-POWERED DISCOVERY VIA MCP
WEEK 1

Claude connects to each portal through HubSpot's MCP server. It reads every contact, company, deal, ticket, property, workflow, and integration — across all portals — and builds a complete map of your entire data landscape. What used to take weeks of manual spreadsheet work happens in hours. Claude flags duplicate contacts, identifies property conflicts, and documents what each portal contains, automatically.

How it works: MCP (Model Context Protocol) gives Claude secure, read-only access to your HubSpot data. You ask "Show me all custom properties across Portal 1 and Portal 2" — Claude pulls both, compares them, and highlights where field names differ for the same data. No manual exports. No spreadsheets.

CLAUDE + MCP
03
AUDIT & MAP
WEEK 1-2

Claude takes the MCP discovery data and generates every deliverable for this phase automatically: the property mapping table (which fields match, which conflict), the duplicate contact count (email-matched across portals), the integration inventory, and the email domain audit. The partner reviews Claude's output and validates the findings with your team leads.

CLAUDE + MCP PARTNER REVIEW
04
CHOOSE TARGET PORTAL
WEEK 2

Partner presents a recommendation with scored decision matrix. Leadership decides: keep the biggest portal, keep the best-configured, or start fresh. The MCP audit data feeds directly into the scoring.

PARTNER LEADS LEADERSHIP DECIDES
05
SANDBOX TESTING
WEEK 3-4

Run a full test migration in HubSpot's sandbox. Zero production risk. The partner runs the Datawarehouse.io migrator tool. After migration, Claude connects to the sandbox via MCP and cross-checks: "Do the record counts match the source? Are associations intact? Did all properties come across?" — automated verification instead of manual spot-checking.

PARTNER EXECUTES CLAUDE VALIDATES
06
PRE-MIGRATION PREP
WEEK 4-5

Configure Business Units, set up email domains (DKIM/SPF/DMARC), rebuild reports and dashboards, install deduplication tools, export safety backups. Set a freeze date — all teams stop creating records in old portals.

PARTNER CONFIGURES TEAMS FREEZE
07
EXECUTE MIGRATION
WEEK 5-6

Migrate data portal by portal. Verify record counts after each. Run deduplication (email-match contacts, domain-match companies). Assign Business Unit tags. Reconnect integrations. Invite users and assign seats.

PARTNER MIGRATES CLAUDE VERIFIES
08
EMAIL WARM-UP & GO-LIVE
WEEK 6-8

Gradually ramp email volume over 4 weeks — start with your most engaged contacts, expand weekly. Monitor bounce rates, spam complaints, and inbox placement daily. Keep old portals read-only for 90 days as your safety net.

PARTNER MONITORS MARKETING SENDS
09
CONNECT UNIFIED MCP
WEEK 8-9

With all data in one portal, replace 3-4 separate MCP connections with a single one. Claude now sees your entire customer base through one lens. Sales asks "Which Product A customers haven't tried Product B?" Marketing asks "Open rate by brand this quarter?" Service asks "Full ticket history for Company X across all products?" One connection, one answer, complete data.

CLAUDE + MCP PARTNER CONFIGURES
10
TEAM TRAINING & ADOPTION
WEEK 9-10

Each team gets hands-on training in the new unified portal. Sales learns cross-sell views. Service learns unified ticket history. Marketing learns Business Unit campaigns. Leadership gets their new dashboards. One migration champion per team for ongoing support.

PARTNER TRAINS ALL TEAMS
ONGOING
PARTNER SUPPORT & OPTIMISATION
MONTH 3+

The partner stays engaged for ongoing optimisation — quarterly workflow reviews, new Business Unit setup as product lines grow, reporting refinement, and strategic CRM advisory. Claude via MCP handles day-to-day queries, data checks, and report generation autonomously.
